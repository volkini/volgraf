Frequently Asked Questions

How do we start a project?

Send a short brief through the quote form — sketches, photos or existing files all work. You'll get a fixed price and timeline within 24 hours. No payment is due until we've agreed on the scope.

How is my design kept confidential?

I'm happy to sign your NDA before you share anything. Your files are never reused, shared or shown in my portfolio without written permission, and I delete them on request after the project closes.

What exactly do I receive at delivery?

Native CAD files plus neutral formats (STEP/IGES), 2D drawings as PDF and DWG/DXF where applicable, and a DFM note. All intellectual property transfers to you on final payment.

How do payments work?

Fixed-price quotes for defined scopes; larger projects are split into milestones so you only pay as agreed stages are delivered. Bank transfer and international payment platforms are both fine.

What if something needs changing after delivery?

Every project includes revisions until the agreed scope is production-ready. If an issue traces back to my design, the fix is free — no time limit, no discussion.
