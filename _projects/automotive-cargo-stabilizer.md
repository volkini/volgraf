A telescoping aluminum system that clamps between trunk walls to keep cargo in place — no drilling, no adhesives.

## Design highlights

- Custom aluminum extrusion profiles with an internal anti-rotation keyway.
- Machined end fittings carrying the clamping loads into rubber contact pads.
- Over-center locking mechanism: one-hand operation, holds preload without creep.
- Sized against a survey of common trunk widths so one SKU covers most vehicles.

## Outcome

The design balances extrusion tooling cost against machining time, landing on a part count and process mix suited to mid-volume production.
