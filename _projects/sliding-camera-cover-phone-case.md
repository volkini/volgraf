A slim phone case with an integrated sliding privacy cover for the camera array, designed specifically for MJF nylon (PA12).

## Design highlights

- Living-rail slide geometry tuned for a consistent detent feel across print orientations.
- Wall thicknesses and clearances set from measured MJF shrinkage, not datasheet nominals.
- The slide snaps in during assembly — no glue, no hardware.

## What you get

The purchase includes print-ready files and a one-page print guide (orientation, expected clearances, post-processing notes).
