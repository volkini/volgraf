Mechanical Design Engineer

CAD design, DFM-driven part development, and manufacturing-ready engineering — from first concept to production floor.

I'm Volkan — a mechanical design engineer who likes designs that survive contact with the production floor. My work spans parametric CAD, DFM and rapid prototyping, with a particular fondness for aluminium: extrusions, machined parts, and the small details that make them manufacturable. If a part is easy to make, easy to assemble and hard to break, I call it done.
